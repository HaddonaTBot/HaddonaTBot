"Uku" and Me

4-31-19

Whats a "Uku" you may ask? It stands for Ukulele, I got this small instrument a few years back ans shes been by my side ever since then.
I've nicknamed this little instrument "Phillis", why? I'm not sure really, just fit and ever since then I've stuck with it. Do I know any songs on it? Not really, I know a little of "I'm Yours" by Jason Mraz, and a few chords from "The Judge" by Twenty One Pilots. Also I've began writing some short fun like strums that I'm leading to put together for a short song. If youre a young musician looking for a fun instrument to start out with then I highly recomend you get your own "Uku".
